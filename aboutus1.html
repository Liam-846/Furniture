ABOUT US

Royal-Family Industry Co.Ltd established in Yangon since 2015 is the professional manufacturing and distributing company of bedding accessories and modern pressed wood furniture. It is located in No.25, Yadanar Street, South Oakalar Township, Yangon. We offer variety of products which can meet your multifarious demands which includes all sorts of Office furniture, hotel bedroom furniture, public area furniture, sofa, school furniture, restaurant furniture and others. In addition, you can also buy a complete selection of wedding furniture for couples in one place. We adhere to the management principles of "quality first, customer first " since the establishment of the company and always do our best to satisfy the potential needs of our customers.

OUR VALUE

  • Customer Obsession:

    Customers are at the center of whatever we do at Royal-Family. We ensure that we get the right customer solution in all our initiatives. We establish a long-term relationship with every customer and aim to delight them in every interaction. We aim to set the global benchmark for customer happiness scores.

  • Honesty & Transparency:

    We are honest, ethical, and trustworthy in the way we live life. We hold the highest standards of corporate governance in all our activities. We communicate transparently with all our stakeholders. When we make mistakes, we are honest and upfront about owning up to them.

  • Action Orientation:

    We have a bias for action. We empower our teams to take fast and well informed decisions. We continuously iterate and learn from our mistakes. Scale and robustness are built in as we move along.

  • Stepping Up:

    We take charge, go the extra mile, and think differently to find innovative solutions. When in doubt, we push ourselves harder to solve newer challenges and get better solutions.

  • Efficiency:

    We are here to build a long-term sustainable business. We aim to do more with less and focus on zero waste. We believe that a self-sustaining business will be responsible and solve customer problems in the right manner.
